Meditation

First developed in the Indian Veda texts, meditation is a technique that involves training the mind to focus and achieve a state of relaxation and inner peace. It has been shown to reduce stress, anxiety, depression, and improve overall well-being and cognitive function.

Box Breathing

1.Inhale for 4 seconds.
 2.Hold the breath for 4 seconds.
 3.Exhale for 4 seconds.
 4.Hold the breath for 4 seconds.

Do Nothing Meditation

1.Pause. Stop whatever you’re doing and just pause.
2.Breathe. Even if all you have time for is a deep inhale and a slow exhale, that’s a very potent moment.
3.Repeat this type of momentary break throughout the day, as often as needed.
