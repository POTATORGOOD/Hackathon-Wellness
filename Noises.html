Sound Hues

Sound hues describes the practice of naming different noises after colors. The color of the noise relates to the power spectrum of a noise signal, or the frequencies of the noise. Some noises like pink noise and white noise help sleeping difficulties and sleep disorders like insomnia and improves sleep relaxation.
